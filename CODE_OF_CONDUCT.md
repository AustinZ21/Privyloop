# Code of Conduct

PrivyLoop should be a constructive place to collaborate on privacy tooling, product design, and applied AI workflows.

Expected behavior:

- be respectful and practical
- keep feedback specific and technical
- assume good intent
- help others understand the repo's current scope and limits

Unacceptable behavior:

- harassment or abusive language
- bad-faith arguments
- personal attacks
- derailing issues or PRs with unrelated promotion

Maintainers may remove comments, close threads, or block participants who repeatedly violate these expectations.
